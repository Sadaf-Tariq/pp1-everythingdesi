Our Speciality

Biryani

Mouthwatering, traditional Pakistani and Indian flavor, made with Pakistani/Indian spices, rice, and meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, and fish), or without meat with eggs and potatoes. Our chefs make biryani distinct and authentic with their original recipe!!!

Kebab

Consist of cut up/ground meat, and various other accompaniments. kebabs are typically cooked on a skewer over a fire, some are oven-baked in a pan. The traditional meat for kebabs is most often lamb meat, beef and chicken. Our chefs have the most authentic recipe to give you the taste of sub-continent!!!

Dessert

These desi desserts are usually fried made with milk, sugar or condensed milk, flavoured with almonds and pistachios, spiced with cardamon, nutmeg, cloves and black pepper, and decorated with nuts, or with gold or silver leaf. Our chefs make them more delicious and sweet with love and devotion!!!
